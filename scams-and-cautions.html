Scams and Rental Cautions

Always proceed with caution when renting. Pepe Deals only provides links to verified listings, but scams exist in the rental market.

Tips to Stay Safe:

  • **Never** send money via wire transfer, cashier's check, or gift card.
  • **Always** tour the property in person before signing a lease or paying a deposit.
  • **Verify** the identity of the landlord or property manager.

If a deal seems too good to be true, it probably is. Our goal is to connect you with legitimate offers, but final verification is your responsibility.
